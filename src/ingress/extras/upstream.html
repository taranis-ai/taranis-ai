Backend temporarily unavailable

The ingress reached your deployment, but the Taranis AI backend couldn’t successfully complete this request. This is commonly caused by a rollout in progress, a pod/service not being ready yet, or a backend dependency being slow/unhealthy.

Most common in Kubernetes / Compose

  • Rollout / scaling: pods updating, new replica warming up, image pull, init containers running.
  • Readiness not met: service has no ready endpoints (probe failing, app booting, migrations).
  • CrashLoop / OOM: container restarts before it can serve traffic.
  • Dependencies: DB / Redis / queue unreachable or saturated.

Status-specific hint

  • Seeing 502 usually means the upstream responded “weirdly” (restart/reset/not-ready behind a proxy).
  • Seeing 504 usually means the upstream didn’t respond in time (slow boot, slow DB, overloaded workers).
